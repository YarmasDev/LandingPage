A New Brand bleeding with High ACoS and No Traction—Then Up 1,166% in Sales in Just 6 Months
The Brand

LGM Soil Amendments is a family-owned business specializing in premium soil amendments for gardening and commercial applications.

Despite a strong physical-market reputation, LGM SOIL entered Amazon with no advertising history and limited digital visibility. The category is dominated by established competitors with thousands of reviews, making it difficult for new entries to earn impressions, gain relevance, or sustain profitable CPCs without a strategic foundation.

The Problem

At launch on Amazon, LGM SOIL struggled with low organic rankings, limited visibility, and weak conversion rates. With no previous Sponsored Products history, there was no keyword data to guide targeting or bidding.

Early campaigns showed an ACoS around 38% and inconsistent impressions, and the account lacked the historical performance signals needed to compete for top-of-search placements.

Increasing spend aggressively at this stage would have raised costs without improving profitability, so the brand needed a relevance-focused, controlled, and data-driven launch strategy.

The Solution

The strategy centered on building a strong foundation before scaling. The team focused on:

  • Relevance-first optimization: controlled bids and consistent exposure to build keyword authority and improve organic ranking.
  • Gradual bid reductions: as relevance grew, bids were lowered to reduce CPC while maintaining traffic and sales momentum.
  • Tight keyword selection: new keywords were added only from converting search terms, ensuring proven purchase intent.
  • Top-of-search campaigns: Sponsored Products campaigns targeted premium placements for core soil-related terms to capture high-intent traffic.
  • Dayparting: starting in August, bids were raised only during peak converting hours and days to improve efficiency and reduce ACoS.

All growth came from Sponsored Products alone, with no changes to the catalog and no Sponsored Brands, Videos, or Display.

The Results

Within six months, the strategy produced clear performance gains:

  • Revenue growth: monthly sales increased from $1.2K in May to $15.2K in August—a 1,166% increase driven by better ranking and optimized ads.
  • ACoS improvement: starting in the high 30s, ACoS dropped to 16–19% consistently by October.
  • Lower CPC: bids decreased as relevance increased, reducing cost-per-click and protecting margins.
  • Stronger presence: improved ranking and consistent impressions led to more organic visibility and repeat conversions.

LGM SOIL's launch shows how relevance-driven optimization, disciplined bidding, and strategic dayparting can transform a new brand in a competitive category—unlocking significant growth without overspending or expanding the product line.

From losing -$100,000 Per Month to Add Six Figures in New Monthly Revenue While Dropping ACoS 36%

The Brand

Quick & Clean has been an established Amazon brand for several years, known for its specialized cleaning solutions for single-serve coffee machines and kitchen appliances.

Despite strong brand recognition their sales remained stable but flat, and advertising efficiency had declined. To break through the plateau, the brand needed a complete performance reset—one capable of protecting its branded visibility, restoring profitability, and unlocking scalable revenue growth.

The Problem

Before management began, Quick & Clean's advertising structure showed clear inefficiencies. ACoS and TACoS were elevated, keyword relevancy was weak, and the account lacked a structured keyword architecture. CPCs were high across core terms, and branded queries were not protected—allowing competitors to intercept valuable traffic.

Sponsored Brands and Sponsored Display were underutilized, while duplicated targetings and fragmented data caused unnecessary spend.

Although sales velocity remained strong, the PPC system could not support scalable growth. The performance trend shifted only when the restructuring process started.

The Solution

A complete full-funnel PPC rebuild was deployed to restore efficiency, defend branded space, and unlock incremental growth. Key actions included:

  • Structural Rebuild: Isolated high-performing keywords and ASINs into dedicated campaigns for better bid control, cleaner data, and improved placement profitability.
  • Audience & Keyword Expansion: Launching new Sponsored Products, Brands, and Display campaigns to reach untapped audiences and high-value keyword clusters.
  • Brand Protection: Creating keyword- and ASIN-level defense campaigns to prevent competitor conquesting on branded searches.
  • N-Gram Expansion: Using N-gram insights to identify strong converting terms and activate new phrase-match and long-tail campaigns.
  • Targeting Cleanup: Removing irrelevant or duplicated targets to improve traffic quality and budget efficiency.
  • Weekly Optimization & Dayparting: Continuously refining bids and placements while concentrating spend during peak converting hours.
  • Listing Optimization & Rank Tracking: Enhancing listings for better conversions and monitoring organic rankings to protect key positions.

The Results

The improvements were immediate and clearly reflected in Amazon's sales data:

  • +$100,000 in New Monthly Revenue (within 3 months): Growth accelerated right at onboarding, surpassing all previous sales ceilings.
  • Significant ACoS Reduction: Stronger relevance and controlled bidding sharply lowered acquisition costs.
  • Lower TACoS & Better Organic Rank: Higher-quality traffic and improved rankings strengthened long-term profitability.
  • Reduced CPCs: Refined segmentation and bid optimization lowered CPCs while increasing impressions.
  • Higher Conversion Rates: Listing enhancements and more qualified traffic boosted conversion consistency.
  • Expanded Category Presence: Long-tail targeting, new audiences, and brand protection increased overall impression share efficiently.

Quick & Clean shifted from a plateauing account to a scalable, predictable revenue engine—adding over $100,000 in monthly revenue while reducing advertising costs.

A Brand Stuck Spinning Its Wheels: 34.66% ACoS, Weak Returns, No Clear Path—Until +607% Sales and 10.89% ACoS in 6 Months
The Brand

Bifenthreen is a small but specialized pest-control brand offering Bifenthrin Plus-C, a professional-grade yet family-safe liquid insecticide designed for both indoor and outdoor use.

Despite having a strong value proposition, the brand entered one of Amazon's most competitive categories—dominated by established players with extensive review depth and aggressive Sponsored Products strategies. The account was active but underperforming, with inconsistent visibility and inefficient campaign structure.

The Problem

Before optimization, the Amazon account struggled with:

  • 34.66% ACoS, too high for the category
  • Weak keyword relevancy and inflated CPCs
  • No strategic keyword architecture or segmentation
  • Underperforming campaigns unable to identify converting signals
  • Low conversion rates due to irrelevant search terms

Without a data-driven structure, any attempt to scale would only increase spend without improving conversions, ranking, or profitability.

The Solution

We executed a complete PPC rebuild focused on rapid data collection and precise optimization:

  • Launched 85+ campaigns to quickly gather conversion data across keywords, ASINs, and audiences.
  • Adjusted bids strategically to strengthen performance on proven high-intent targets.
  • Applied daily negative keywords to remove irrelevant traffic and protect spend.
  • Pruned inefficient campaigns, consolidating budget toward profitable segments.
  • Used N-gram and ASIN-level analysis to uncover the specific terms and competitors that consistently converted.
The Results

In six months, Bifenthreen achieved a complete turnaround:

  • +607% increase in monthly sales (from $10,152 to $71,787)
  • ACoS reduced from 34.66% to 10.89%
  • CPC lowered by 64% (from $1.48 to $0.54)
  • Higher conversion rates thanks to cleaner traffic and targeted bidding
  • Stronger organic ranking across core pest-control terms
  • A fully profitable, scalable PPC structure ready for continued growth

Bifenthreen's results show how a data-driven, relevance-focused PPC strategy can turn a weak account into a profitable, scalable engine.
